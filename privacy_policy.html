Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 2024

Zutual ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by Zutual. This Privacy Policy applies to our app, Zutual, and its associated services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you use our app, we may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, username, and password.
  • Profile Information: Profile picture, bio, interests, and other profile details.
  • Location Information: GPS location, if you provide permission.
  • Interaction Information: Likes, dislikes, messages, and other interactions.

1.2 Usage Information

We collect information about your use of the app, including but not limited to:

  • App usage data, such as the features you use and the time spent on the app.
  • Device information, such as your device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our services: We use your information to operate and provide the features and functionalities of our app.
  • To improve our services: We use your information to understand how our app is used and to make improvements.
  • To communicate with you: We use your information to send you updates, notifications, and other information related to your use of the app.
  • To ensure security: We use your information to monitor and protect the security of our app and its users.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties except in the following circumstances:

  • With your consent: We may share your information if you give us permission.
  • For legal reasons: We may share your information if required by law or to respond to legal processes.
  • To protect our rights: We may share your information to protect the rights, property, or safety of Zutual, our users, or others.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no security system is impenetrable, and we cannot guarantee the security of your data.

5. Your Choices

You have the following choices regarding your information:

  • Access and Update: You can access and update your personal information through the app settings.
  • Delete Your Account: You can delete your account and associated data by following the instructions on our Delete Account page.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@zutual.com.
