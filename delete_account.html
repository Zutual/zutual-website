Delete Your Account

If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, you can do so directly from within the app. Please follow the instructions below:

  1. Open the app and log in to your account.
  2. Navigate to the "Settings" icon in the profile page.
  3. Find and select the "Delete Account" option.
  4. Confirm your choice to delete your account.
